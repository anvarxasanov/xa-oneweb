A landing page is usually a single-page website focused on one product, service, or campaign with a clear conversion goal. A corporate website has multiple pages and is better for presenting a company, its services, team, portfolio, and contact information in a more complete way.

You should choose an e-commerce website when you need to sell products or services online, accept payments, manage orders, and allow customers to browse a catalog. It is the right option for businesses that need a full online store experience.

A blog website is designed for publishing regular content like articles, news, guides, or updates. It works well for personal brands, educators, agencies, and businesses that want to grow through SEO and content marketing.
